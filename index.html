About Me

I'm a full-stack software engineer specializing in web development using the MERN and MEAN stacks.

I built my first website when I was in middle school, experimenting with HTML and CSS just for fun. This simple curiosity grew with time, and today, I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Alexandria University.

I gained hands-on experience through personal projects and internship programs. It's where I learned how to work with modern web technologies and build production-ready web applications.

When I build applications, I focus on creating products that people would actually want to use. Because good software should work well and feel good to use.

I'm always learning and looking for the next thing to build. If you think we could build something good together, reach out to me on any of my socials.
